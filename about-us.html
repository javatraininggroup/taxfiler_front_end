About Us What We Are

Besttaxfiler (BTF) is a team of experienced professionals – from Tax and Finance Industry with core expertise and practicing skill to represent client needs. We have been helping with U.S. Tax Returns filing for over 15 years, additionally expanded to provide book keeping and payroll services.

At Besttaxfiler, we specialize in the preparation of U.S. Tax Returns for Non Resident Indian’s working in US (H1B/L1) as well as Indians whose purpose of visit is Education (OPT/CPT).

Besttaxfiler provides most economical services to help our clients derive highest value. Our procedures are designed to make Tax Return preparation easy and painless. Besttaxfiler online process takes away the time and cost of visiting tax firms to file your returns. Once registered, clients are provided with required services.
